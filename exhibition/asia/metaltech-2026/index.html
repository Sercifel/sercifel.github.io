METALTECH 2026: The Gateway to Smart Manufacturing in the ASEAN Region

May 20, 2026

METALTECH 2026, Malaysia’s premier metalworking and machine tool exhibition, takes place at MITEC in Kuala Lumpur alongside AUTOMEX.

METALTECH 2026, Malaysia’s premier international exhibition for metalworking, machine tools, and manufacturing technology, is scheduled to take place at the Malaysia International Trade and Exhibition Centre (MITEC) in Kuala Lumpur. As the largest event of its kind in the region, METALTECH, held alongside AUTOMEX (the International Automation Technology Exhibition), serves as a critical platform for the rapid industrialization of Southeast Asia.

The 2026 edition focuses on the transition toward Industry 4.0, emphasizing automation, precision engineering, and sustainable production technologies designed to support Malaysia's growing role in global semiconductor and automotive supply chains.

Event Logistics and Venue

  • Dates: May 2026 (Official specific dates to be finalized by Informa Markets)

  • Venue: Malaysia International Trade and Exhibition Centre (MITEC) (8, Jalan Dutamas 2, 50480 Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia)

  • Scale: The event typically hosts over 1,500 exhibiting companies and attracts more than 20,000 trade professionals from over 40 countries.

  • Strategic Access: Situated in Kuala Lumpur, the exhibition provides a central hub for visitors from Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, and Vietnam.

Core Exhibition Scopes

The exhibition floor is meticulously organized to cover the entire manufacturing value chain:

  1. Machine Tools & Metalworking: Featuring the latest CNC machining centers, EDM machines, and high-precision lathes.

  2. Sheet Metal Technology: Dedicated to laser cutting, punching, and bending solutions for the electronics and infrastructure sectors.

  3. Metrology & Quality Assurance: Showcasing 3D scanning, CMM, and digital inspection tools for high-accuracy manufacturing.

  4. Hardware & Tools: Focusing on industrial-grade cutting tools, lubricants, and workshop accessories.

High-Precision Material Preparation: The Role of Everising

A critical component of the manufacturing workflow showcased at METALTECH is the initial material preparation and heavy-duty cutting. In this sector, Everising, a globally recognized bandsaw manufacturer, is expected to present its advanced sawing solutions.

Specializing in both circular saws and high-performance bandsaws, Everising’s participation highlights the demand in the ASEAN market for automated, high-speed cutting technology that can handle diverse materials, from aerospace alloys to heavy structural steel. Their systems are integral to minimizing material waste and optimizing the subsequent CNC machining stages, aligning with the "Green Manufacturing" initiatives prominent in the 2026 exhibition.

Strategic Value: Why Attend METALTECH 2026?

1. The Southeast Asian Hub for Global Nearshoring

As multinational corporations diversify their manufacturing bases, Malaysia has become a key destination for high-tech industries. METALTECH 2026 provides international investors and local manufacturers a direct opportunity to source technology that meets global quality standards.

2. The Synergy of AUTOMEX

The co-location with AUTOMEX allows attendees to explore not just standalone machinery, but fully integrated automated systems. This includes:

  • Industrial Robotics: Autonomous material handling and robotic welding.

  • IIoT Solutions: Real-time data monitoring for production lines to improve OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness).

3. Technical Seminars and Knowledge Exchange

The exhibition features a series of high-level seminars led by industry experts. These sessions cover critical regional topics such as the implementation of the National Industry4WRD policy and the adoption of low-carbon manufacturing practices to comply with international ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) requirements.

Planning and Professional Participation

Technical managers and distributors are encouraged to utilize the digital matchmaking tools to maximize their presence at MITEC.

  • Registration: Trade visitors must register through the official METALTECH portal. Early registration is advised to secure seats for the specialized technical forums.

  • B2B Matchmaking: The "Business Matching Program" facilitates targeted meetings between technology providers, such as bandsaw experts from Everising, and regional buyers looking for specific production upgrades.
