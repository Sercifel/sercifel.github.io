NPE 2027: The Plastics Show Returns to Orlando with the "Next Is Now" Theme

May 04, 2027

NPE 2027 runs May 3-7, 2027 at the Orange County Convention Center in Orlando, bringing the full plastics supply chain together under the official theme "Next Is Now" with a focus on innovation, sustainability, and business growth.

Event Overview

NPE 2027: The Plastics Show will take place May 3-7, 2027 at the Orange County Convention Center (OCCC) in Orlando, Florida, and it is positioned as the largest plastics trade show in the Americas. The official site frames the event as a global marketplace where the entire plastics value chain comes together, with exhibitors and attendees spanning equipment suppliers, material suppliers, processors, and recyclers. For a neutral industry audience, NPE can be described as a full supply chain platform where manufacturing leaders evaluate new processes, materials, automation, and sustainability solutions that drive competitiveness and compliance. The show is owned and produced by the Plastics Industry Association (PLASTICS), which underscores its role as an industry anchor event rather than a regional marketplace, and the official communication highlights scale markers such as more than 2,200 exhibiting companies and 51,000+ attendees, with participation from more than 130 countries.

2027 Theme: "Next Is Now" and Strategic Focus

The official press release announces "NEXT IS NOW" as the theme for NPE 2027, framing the event around adoption over aspiration and positioning the plastics industry as actively shaping its future rather than waiting for it. The release notes that the show will be defined by its impact on business growth, innovation, sustainability, and the perception of plastics, and it emphasizes that the industry is at an inflection point driven by emerging technologies, evolving market expectations, and new sustainability regulations. In practical terms, this theme signals a shift toward deployment-ready solutions, with a focus on circularity, supply chain resilience, and climate goals rather than purely conceptual ideas. For buyers, this suggests an event designed to connect strategy with execution; for suppliers, it indicates a demand for measurable outcomes and real-world applications that can demonstrate progress on regulatory and customer requirements.

Industry Scope and Cross-Sector Reach

NPE’s official positioning highlights that the full plastics supply chain will gather in one place, which matters because innovations in plastics rarely occur in isolation. Material decisions affect processing, processing affects recyclability, and recyclability affects brand and regulatory outcomes, so a cross-sector forum is essential for aligning technical and commercial priorities. The official site emphasizes the presence of technology zones and industry segments such as automotive, building and construction, consumer products, medical, and packaging, which signals that NPE is designed to support buyers who need to evaluate solutions across multiple application markets. The scale and scope also imply that NPE is a key venue for benchmarking global trends, identifying supplier alternatives, and validating new materials or production methods in front of a broad set of technical and procurement stakeholders.

Example Supplier Fit: Great Eastern Resins

If you want to include a company example aligned to NPE’s materials and formulation focus, Great Eastern Resins Industrial is a reasonable fit to mention in a neutral way. Based on the company’s official site, it focuses on adhesive and related adjuvant products and plastic raw materials, which map directly to the material innovation and supply chain themes emphasized by NPE. A short neutral insertion could be: "Great Eastern Resins Industrial Co., Ltd. focuses on adhesive and related adjuvant products and plastic raw materials, which aligns with NPE’s materials and formulation scope and the industry’s push for performance, compliance, and sustainability across the value chain." This keeps the mention factual, aligns with the show’s category coverage, and avoids implying confirmed participation unless you later provide exhibitor confirmation.

Buyer and Supplier Value

For buyers, NPE 2027 provides a consolidated venue to compare equipment, materials, and sustainability solutions that influence manufacturing cost, product performance, and compliance readiness. The "Next Is Now" positioning and the emphasis on circularity and resilience suggest that purchasing decisions will be evaluated not only on technical performance but also on their ability to support regulatory and customer expectations. For suppliers, the event offers access to a global audience of decision makers who are actively looking for innovations that can move from pilot to deployment, which is critical in a market where competitive advantage increasingly depends on speed to implement and proof of measurable outcomes. The scale of NPE also supports partnership building across the value chain, making it a practical environment for both product discovery and strategic collaboration.
