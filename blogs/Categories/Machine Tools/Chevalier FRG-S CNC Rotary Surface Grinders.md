# Elevating Precision: Chevalier's FRG-S Series CNC Rotary Surface Grinders

![Chevalier's FRG-S Series CNC Rotary Surface Grinders](https://www.chevaliertw.com/storage/media/media-center/distributor-newsletter/202508_newsletter/Chevalier%202025Q3%20Newsletter%20Get%20Ready%20for%20Something%20Big%20More%20selection%20for%20CNC%20Rotary%20Surface%20Grinders_files/95e64bf5-2913-3f30-b62d-e448f76ad82a.jpg)

Chevalier's **[FRG-S series CNC rotary surface grinders](https://www.chevaliertw.com/en/products/FRG-S-Series-SMART-iControl)** set a new standard in high-precision manufacturing for industries like aerospace, automotive, energy, medical, and semiconductors. These machines combine robust engineering with SMART iControl technology for efficient, heavy-duty grinding.​​

## Advanced Machine Design

The series features a symmetrical, ribbed high-rigidity column and a fully supported, low center-of-gravity base for exceptional stability. A fully enclosed hydrostatic rotary table ensures smooth operation under heavy loads, while double-door access and efficient swarf drainage simplify maintenance. Models support versatile modes including surface, step, stair, and profile grinding.​​

## Standout FRG-1000S Model

![FRG-1000S](https://www.chevaliertw.com/storage/media/products/grinding/FRG-400S600S/FRG-1000S_202507.png)
The FRG-1000S, the largest variant, introduces a wide-span column design with dual Z-axis drive, handling max loads up to 1,200 kg, grinding radii to 525 mm, and heights to 400 mm. Its 15-18 kW spindle and Ø1,000 mm table at 10-80 rpm excel in demanding applications.[](https://www.chevaliertw.com/en/products/FRG-S-Series-SMART-iControl)​

## SMART iControl Technology

![SMART iControl by FALCON MACHINE TOOLS](https://www.chevaliertw.com/storage/media/smart%2B/smart-icontrol/smart-icontrol-main.jpg)
[SMART iControl](https://www.chevaliertw.com/en/page-categories/smart-icontrol) revolutionizes operation with conversational programming on a 10.4" touchscreen, replacing G-code via 3D simulations for intuitive setup. It processes 2,000 blocks/second, supports 8 axes (4 simultaneous), and includes SMART Dressing for constant wheel contact, adaptive feed rates, and vibration suppression.

## Automation Integration

Compact footprints (e.g., 2,800 x 2,800 mm for FRG-400S/600S) and spacious interiors enable seamless robot arm integration for unmanned loading/unloading. Ethernet iMCS and SRI IO modules facilitate remote monitoring and automation links, ideal for smart factories.

## Real-World Automation Examples

While FRG-S-specific cases are emerging, Chevalier's similar lines like [FMG-1632CNC-HD](https://www.chevaliertw.com/en/products/FMG-1632CNC-HD) demonstrate automation success in aerospace bracket grinding, integrating robots for precision batches and manpower savings—directly applicable to FRG-S via its compatible controls.

This series drives productivity with minimal setup, superior finishes, and Industry 4.0 readiness, positioning it as a top choice for precision grinding innovation.
