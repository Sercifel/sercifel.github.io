# Driving the Green Transition: High Efficiency and Precision with Victor Taichung’s VαⅢ-50 and VαⅢ-350

![](https://b2bmachinery.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/unnamed.png)
In an era where global climate change is putting immense pressure on the plastics industry to decarbonize, manufacturers are looking for solutions that balance high performance with environmental responsibility. **Victor Taichung Machinery Works** is leading this charge with its **VαⅢ series of [all-electric injection molding machines](https://injection-molding.victortaichung.com/shop/category/all-electric-injection-molding-machine-gs-series-35)**, designed specifically for high efficiency, precision, and sustainability.

Today, we take a closer look at two flagship models in this series: the **VαⅢ-50** and the **VαⅢ-350**, which together represent the versatile range of Victor Taichung’s smart manufacturing capabilities.

**The VαⅢ Series: 50% Energy Savings and High-Speed Precision**

The defining feature of the VαⅢ series is its remarkable **50% energy saving** compared to traditional hydraulic machines. This architecture significantly reduces the carbon footprint of production, making it an ideal choice for businesses pursuing a "green transition".

Beyond energy savings, the series is engineered for high-performance output, featuring:

• **High-Speed Injection:** Speeds of up to **500 mm/sec**.

• **Rapid Acceleration:** Acceleration response capability exceeding **3.5 G**.

• **Cleanliness:** Perfect for **cleanroom environments** required in medical and semiconductor sectors.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**VαⅢ-50: The Precision Specialist**

The **VαⅢ-50** is a 50-ton (55.11 U.S. ton) powerhouse designed for the most demanding high-precision tasks. It is particularly well-suited for the **healthcare, precision optics, and electronics industries**.

• **Compact & Precise:** With a tie-bar distance of **360 x 310 mm** and a mold thickness range of **150-350 mm**, it offers a stable platform for small, intricate parts.

• **Flexible Injection:** Depending on the unit (C or D), it offers screw diameters from **22 mm to 32 mm**, achieving shot weights between **32g and 76g** (PS).

• **Application:** Its ability to operate in cleanrooms makes it the go-to choice for high-quality medical plastic products.

**VαⅢ-350: Scaling Up Efficiency**

For manufacturers requiring higher clamping forces without sacrificing the benefits of an all-electric system, the **VαⅢ-350** offers a robust solution. This 350-ton machine is capable of handling larger-scale production while maintaining the energy efficiency of the VαⅢ line.

• **Robust Clamping:** Features a **350-ton** clamping force and a generous platen dimension of **1090 x 1140 mm**.

• **High Capacity:** It supports a maximum shot weight of up to **851g (PS)** and an injection capacity of **908 cm³** (Unit K), making it versatile for transportation equipment, packaging, and home appliances.

• **Stability:** Even at this larger scale, the machine maintains the precision and stability required for high-yield manufacturing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Smart Solutions for the Future of Manufacturing**

Both the VαⅢ-50 and VαⅢ-350 are equipped with Victor Taichung’s latest **Green Intelligent Solutions** to further optimize production:

1. **AI and Digital Twin Integration:** Using **mold flow analysis software**, users can create a "digital twin" to simulate the molding process virtually. This predicts product quality and **shortens trial molding times**, preventing production delays.

2. **Molding Technology Guidance System:** This interactive system provides optimal parameter suggestions based on product and mold data, allowing even less experienced operators to master the machine quickly.

3. **Recycled Material UP Solution:** This technology uses specialized barrel designs and **AIOT quality monitoring** to increase the stability and yield of parts made from recycled plastics.

4. **Connectivity (PIS):** Utilizing the **OPCUA international standard**, these machines connect to the Production Information Management platform, enabling real-time data analysis and seamless integration with MES or ERP systems.

**Conclusion**

Whether you need the surgical precision of the **VαⅢ-50** or the high-capacity performance of the **VαⅢ-350**, Victor Taichung’s all-electric series provides the tools necessary to thrive in a low-carbon, smart manufacturing future.
