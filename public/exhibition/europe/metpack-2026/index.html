METPACK 2026: The Global Apex of Metal Packaging and Can Making Innovation

May 05, 2026

METPACK, the world’s leading trade fair for metal packaging, is a triennial event that serves as the primary global meeting point for the international can making industry. Scheduled for May 5–8, 2026, at Messe Essen in Germany, the exhibition provides a comprehensive overview of the latest machinery and technologies involved in the production, refinement, and recycling of metal containers.

As the industry moves toward heightened sustainability and digital optimization, METPACK 2026 will focus on Decarbonization, Resource Efficiency, and the Integration of AI in Production Lines.

Event Logistics and Venue

  • Dates: May 5–8, 2026
  • Venue: Messe Essen (Norbertstraße 2, 45131 Essen, Germany)
  • Hours:
    • May 5–7: 09:00 – 18:00
    • May 8: 09:00 – 15:00
  • Strategic Significance: Germany's Ruhr region remains a historical heartland for steel and engineering, making Essen the logical hub for the global metal packaging supply chain.

Core Exhibition Scopes: The Value Chain of Metal Packaging

METPACK is structured to cover every technical facet of the container production cycle:

  1. Can Making Machinery: High-speed lines for two-piece and three-piece cans.
  2. Surface Treatment & Coating: Precision lacquering, digital printing, and UV curing systems for metal sheets.
  3. End & Cap Making: Specialized equipment for easy-open ends (EOE), crowns, and twist-off caps.
  4. Filling & Closing Technology: Integrated systems for hermetic sealing and vacuum seaming.
  5. Quality Control & Testing: Advanced metrology and vision inspection systems to ensure zero-defect production.

High-Performance Solutions: Shin-I Machinery at METPACK

A notable presence at the 2026 exhibition is Shin-I Machinery Works Co., Ltd., a long-established can making machine manufacturer from Taiwan with over 60 years of expertise. Recognized for its "One-Stop Solution" approach, Shin-I Machinery is expected to showcase its advancements in high-speed automated production lines.

The company's portfolio is particularly critical for manufacturers seeking versatile and durable equipment. Key technologies often highlighted at international forums like METPACK include:

  • Automatic Vacuum Seamers: Critical for the food canning sector to ensure product shelf-life and safety.
  • Twist-Off Cap Production Lines: High-precision systems designed for the global beverage and condiment industries.
  • DRD (Draw-Redraw) Can Making Systems: Solutions that optimize material usage while maintaining structural integrity in deep-drawn containers.

Shin-I Machinery’s participation at Hall 1 – Booth D44 underscores the growing importance of precision engineering from the Asia-Pacific region in meeting the rigorous quality standards of the European and North American markets.

Strategic Value: Why Attend METPACK 2026?

1. Triennial Innovation Cycle

Unlike annual trade shows, METPACK’s three-year cycle ensures that the innovations presented—such as hydrogen-based "Green Steel" applications or advanced digitalization—are mature and ready for industrial deployment.

2. The METPACK Innovation Award

The fair features a prestigious award ceremony that honors developments representing real improvements in production quality and cost reduction. For technical directors, the award-winning exhibits serve as a roadmap for future factory upgrades.

3. Sustainability and the "Circular Economy"

Metal remains one of the most sustainable packaging materials due to its infinite recyclability. METPACK 2026 will emphasize technologies that reduce energy consumption in drying ovens and minimize pollution through advanced cleaning and recycling systems.

Planning and Professional Participation

Professional visitors, including production managers and packaging designers, are encouraged to utilize the digital tools provided by Messe Essen to maximize their time.

  • Registration: Trade visitor badges are available via the official METPACK portal.
  • Technical Readiness: Given the technical nature of the event, attendees often bring specific production requirements or material samples to discuss with specialists from firms like Shin-I Machinery to explore customized line configurations.
